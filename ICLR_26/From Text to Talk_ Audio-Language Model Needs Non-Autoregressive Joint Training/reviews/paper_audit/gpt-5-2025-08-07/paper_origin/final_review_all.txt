The approach includes a modality-aware attention pattern (Figure 2b) and three training strategies to mitigate train‚Äìtest discrepancies (BANOM, PPM, SST; Section 3.3/3.4). Inference uses block-wise diffusion (Algorithm 1; Figure 3b) with parallel audio span synthesis and alternating AR‚ÜîNAR decoding (Section 3.5). Experiments on Audio-QA and ASR report gains over AR or NAR-only baselines, with ablations validating each strategy (Table 1; Sections 4.2‚Äì4.3).Strengths
